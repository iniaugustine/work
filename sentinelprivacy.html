PRIVACY POLICY

The Sentinel Corp. is committed to protecting the privacy and accuracy of to the extent possible, subject to provisions of state and federal law. Other than as required by laws that guarantee the public access to certain types of informations, or other legal instuments that authorize access, personall-identifiable information is not shared. In particular, we do not redistribute or sell personal information collected on our web servers.

Use of Collected Information
  • The Sentinel Corp. may use personal information collected from websites for the purpose of future communication back to online enrollees, inorder to keep you informed of such activities as changes in routine, agents, and/or company policies, but only if you are provided the opportunity to opt out of that type of use.
  • The Sentinel Corp. may use browser-IP-address information and anonymous-browser history to report information about site accesses and for profiling processes. This information is generally used to improve Web presentation and utilization. The company may also use IP address information for troubleshooting purposes.
  • Some of The Sentinel Corp. online activities may require the use of "cookies" in order to deliver web content specific to individual users' interests or keep track of online pruchasing transactions. Sensitive personal information is not stored within cookies.
Distribution of Collected Information
  • The Sentinel Corp. will not disclose, without your consent, your personal information collected about you, except for certain explicit circumstances in which disclosure is required by the law.
  • The Sentinel Corp. will not distribute or sell personal information to third-party organizations.
Individual Choice
  • Individuals who wish to use methods other than online application for services may request by email to the Sentinel Corp. organisations responsible for the website.
Access to Own Information
  • Questions to users' rights to review, modify or delete their previously provided personal information should be directed to the company's organization to which they provided the information. Any disputes will be resolved under existing record regulations applicable to The Sentinel Corp.
Responsibility for External Sites
  • The Sentinel Corp. does not control the content or information practices of external organizations. We recommend you review the statements of these organizations.
